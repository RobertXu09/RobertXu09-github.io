Work-1 Sample 2

I intend my website to be my personal site, where I can put my resume, experience, and projects. Most professional sites are clean and serious, but I don’t want mine to be like that. I am currently interested in game design/development and I really want my website to display a sense of my personality/creativity, so I’m thinking about approaching my website from that angle.

The big dream for my site would be one where navigation of my site can be controlled with WASD or arrow keys to get a character to the links for different pages. Of course, I understand not everyone would be interested in that, so I’ll have to make it clear that simply clicking on links will work as well, or even make a button that transforms the site into a much more legible and simpler format. I’m envisioning a world in a style similarto Super Mario Bros 3 where all my links to subpages like resume, experience, and portfolio have a themed “door” of some sort. This is super ambitious or might even come out as a bad product, but I do want to attempt to make it happen. I have some experience making pixel art with a platform called Aseprite, and although I am not extremely well-versed with JS, I know basic syntax and I think I can google my way there. I can always fall back on just stylizing my pages as a retro game. I’m thinking of a bright, even cute, palette, and so I know I’ll have to pay extra attention to legibility.
